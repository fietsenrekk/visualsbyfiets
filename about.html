Behind the Visuals

One editor. Every frame on purpose.

Visuals by Fiets is the one-person studio of a VFX artist and video editor obsessed with one thing: making people stop scrolling.

I cut music edits, brand teasers and short-form content engineered for TikTok, Reels and Shorts — beat-synced, typography-heavy, VFX-driven. The kind of edit where the punch-in lands exactly when the 808 does.

Next to editing, I design and animate 3D logos: Supreme, Bape, Chrome Hearts, Audemars Piguet and more have gone through the pipeline as concept work — modeled, lit and rendered with cinematic materials. Your mark gets the same treatment.

Everything is built in Premiere Pro, After Effects and 3D software, and I sell the actual project files — because the fastest way to level up your own edits is to open a finished one and see how it ticks.

No agencies, no handoffs, no account managers. You brief me, I cut it, we refine it together. That's why the turnaround is fast and the style is consistent.
